Alexa Spotify Integration

About the App

This is a fun little side project I have done to address the pain point of listening to a new song and having to unlock you phone or switch apps on your device to log into your spotify app and then like the song or add it to a playlist. With this you will be able to ask alexa to dynamically add a new song you have discovered to your like songs or a specific playlist without the cumbersome task of manually doing that.

Authorisation process

In order for the alexa skill to function properly, it needs you to give it permission to access to spotify player playback status so it can retrieve the song you are listening to in real time and then add that to your like songs or a playlist of your choice. NOTE: it will only have access to your playback information it cannot access any other information you don't authorise to, we configure the authourisation scope of the app purely for playback information access only.
